Services

Your items or ours, custom or stock, we can embroider various designs including:

  • Simple Text: Personalized in various fonts and colors, including monograms and initials.
  • Stock Design: Select from thousands of stock designs from our embroidery catalog.
  • Custom Logos: Bring your ideas, sketches and business logos; we can digitize and embroider them on your favorite items.
  • Patches: We can make and sew patches on your garment.
